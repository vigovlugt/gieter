Treat yourself to a quiet getaway in this charming Holiday cottage in an old farmhouse, just 15 km from Orléans and the banks of the Loire. Whether you're on vacation with family, friends or business, La Bergerie welcomes you to a verdant setting, ideal for relaxation and discovery. Situated 24 km from the Château de Meung-sur-Loire, an essential stopover on the Route des Châteaux de la Loire, this house combines modern comfort with authenticity.

Description

Find out more

Description 2026, subject to modifications for 2027: Please refer to the description sheet validated at the time of booking.

On the first floor, you'll enjoy a large 55 m² living room with a comfortable lounge, a cosy dining room and an open, fully-equipped kitchen, ideal for preparing meals in a convivial atmosphere. There are also two shower rooms, one with toilet.

Upstairs, four bedrooms, two with double beds (140x190 cm), one with two single beds (90x190 cm), one with a single bed (90x190 cm) and a separate toilet.

You'll enjoy a private terrace opening onto an enclosed garden, perfect for outdoor meals with its garden furniture, deckchairs and charcoal barbecue. At the front of the property, a portico with swing will delight the youngest, while the older ones will have fun around the ping-pong table.

Private parking is also available.

All-inclusive rates: heating included, bed linen provided and beds made on arrival, bathroom linen included and end-of-stay cleaning included.

A second adjoining Holiday cottage for up to 8 people is also available, ideal for large families or groups.

Arrival from 4 pm to a maximum of 6 pm - Departure at 10 am.

Baby equipment available on request: high chair, Umbrella bed and stair safety gate.
