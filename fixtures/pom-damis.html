Holiday rentals between Pilat and Monts du Lyonnais in St-Romain-en-Jarez, Loire!
Set in a medieval village surrounded by orchards, this Holiday cottage invites you to enjoy an authentic stay in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes region. Hiking in the Pilat Regional Nature Park, strolls in the Giers Valley less than 10 km away, farmers' markets and gourmet discoveries will set the pace for your vacation. Heritage lovers will also appreciate the characterful villages of the Monts du Lyonnais and their unspoilt panoramas.
Back at home, you'll rediscover the charm of an old house, with its stone walls, exposed beams, original staircase and wooden floors, combined with today's simple, functional comforts, ideal for weekends away or vacations with family or friends.

Discover this ideal holiday cottage for 14 people, adjoining the owners' house, designed to combine space, conviviality and comfort. Spanning 220 m² on two levels, it offers spacious accommodation for everyone. The 5 bedrooms are distributed as follows: 1 bed 160, 1 bed 140, 1 bedroom with 1 bed 140 and 1 bed 90, 1 bedroom with 4 beds 90 and 1 bedroom with 3 beds 90. There are also 4 WCs and 3 shower rooms, guaranteeing fluidity and comfort on a daily basis.
On the first floor, the independent, fully-equipped kitchen invites you to prepare delicious meals, while the vast dining room becomes the beating heart of the house, ideal for lively get-togethers and long evenings spent together. Upstairs, the relaxation lounge offers a more hushed space, ideal for conversation or moments of calm.
